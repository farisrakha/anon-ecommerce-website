# Product

## Register
product

## Users
B2B wholesale buyers: independent retailers, multi-brand boutiques, and fashion
buyers placing bulk orders. Professional, time-pressured. They evaluate brands
by how the interface carries itself — credibility is visual before it is verbal.

## Product Purpose
NOCTE is a luxury wholesale storefront. It exists to present the brand to
professional buyers with the same authority and precision as a showroom floor.
Success means buyers trust the brand before they have read a single word.

## Brand Personality
Austere. Architectural. Authoritative. The interface does not perform warmth —
it performs confidence. Every screen should feel like a curated space, not a
catalogue.

## Anti-references
- Generic luxury white: Chanel.com softness — hairline serifs, vast whitespace,
  paper-white grounds. Too ethereal, too consumer.
- SaaS enterprise gray: Salesforce-style dense panels, pill badges, blue CTAs.
  Too corporate, signals off-brand tooling.
- Mass-market e-commerce: Shopify defaults — hero banners, SALE badges, star
  rating clusters, color-accent noise. Too loud.
- Consumer DTC fashion: Farfetch / Matches lifestyle photography, editorial-as-
  decoration, model-shot heroes. Wrong register entirely.

## Design Principles
1. The showroom floor — every surface earns its presence; nothing is there by default
2. Professional confidence — signals authority to buyers who have seen every luxury brand
3. Hierarchy through restraint: contrast and scale carry meaning, never color
4. Typography declares, never decorates — the editorial serif IS the brand voice
5. Zero noise — professional buyers move fast; decoration is friction

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA. Dark-first palette — verify contrast ratios at each surface level.
No specific accommodation requirements stated beyond standard; test for keyboard
navigation and screen reader compatibility as baseline.
